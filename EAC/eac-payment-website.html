Platform Features

A Complete DeFi Rail
Built for East Africa

EACPay combines the battle-tested simplicity of mobile money with the trust guarantees of blockchain — giving individuals and banks a single, tamper-proof payment network.

⛓️
On-Chain Settlement
Every transaction is recorded on Polygon PoS via our EACSettlement smart contract. Immutable, verifiable, and lightning-fast with sub-3-second finality.
👛
Non-Custodial Wallets
Each customer gets their own smart wallet deployed on-chain. Private keys never touch our servers — generated client-side with BIP-39 mnemonics.
💱
Live FX Engine
Rates refresh every 15 minutes from EAC market data. A transparent 0.8% spread on mid-market rates — no hidden fees, ever.
🏦
Multi-Bank Network
10+ pre-integrated EAC correspondent banks across all 8 nations. Automatic intermediary routing when direct corridors don't exist.
🔐
Military-Grade Security
Argon2id password hashing, AES-256-GCM key encryption, EIP-712 typed signing, and HSM-backed bank relayer keys. OWASP Top-10 compliant.
⚡
Real-Time Updates
WebSocket push notifications for transaction confirmations, tamper alerts, and wallet balance changes — no polling required.
Process

From Quote to Confirmation
in Four Steps

Our smart contract signing flow ensures every payment is cryptographically authorised by the sender — with full transparency at every step.

01
Get a Quote
Enter the source currency, destination, and amount. Our FX engine instantly returns a live rate with full fee breakdown — no surprises.
02
Sign & Approve
The backend constructs an EIP-712 typed payload. You sign it locally in your browser using your private key — nothing leaves your device.
03
Broadcast to Chain
Our bank relayer verifies your signature and broadcasts the signed transaction to the Polygon network via the EACSettlement contract.
04
Confirmed & Delivered
The on-chain PaymentConfirmed event triggers a webhook, updating your dashboard in real time. Your recipient's bank is notified instantly.

Smart Contracts

Tamper-Proof by Design,
Audited for Trust

Three production Solidity contracts on Polygon PoS handle every payment, wallet, and settlement — open to inspection by anyone, controlled by no one.

📜
EACSettlement.sol
Core payment contract storing every transaction on-chain. Emits PaymentInitiated, PaymentConfirmed, and PaymentRejected events with full indexing.
🏭
EACWalletFactory.sol
Deploys an ERC-1167 minimal proxy (clone) wallet for each registered customer. Maintains an owner-to-wallet registry on-chain.
💎
EACToken.sol (EAC-USD)
Optional regional stablecoin (6 decimals) used as the settlement intermediary. Mintable only by a Treasury multisig. Pausable for emergency governance.

Security & Compliance

Enterprise Security.
DeFi Transparency.

We never take shortcuts with your money. Every layer — from browser to blockchain — is hardened against attack.

🔑
Client-Side Key Generation
BIP-39 mnemonics generated in-browser. Private keys derived via PBKDF2 (310,000 iterations) and encrypted with AES-256-GCM. Your keys never touch our servers.
🛡️
OWASP Top-10 Hardened
Helmet.js headers, Redis-backed rate limiting (20 req/min on auth), AJV input validation, CORS allowlisting, and append-only audit logging for all write operations.
🔍
Smart Contract Audit
All Solidity contracts analysed with Slither and Mythril. Zero high/critical findings required before mainnet deploy. 100% branch coverage on settlement logic.
🏛️
HSM-Backed Relayer
Bank relayer keys secured in AWS KMS or HashiCorp Vault — never in a plain .env file on production. Secrets injected at runtime via Secrets Manager.
✍️
EIP-712 Typed Signing
Every transaction requires a structured data signature from the sender's wallet. The relayer verifies the signer address before broadcasting — no impersonation possible.
🌐
WCAG 2.1 AA Compliant
Colour contrast, full keyboard navigation, and ARIA labels on every interactive element. Mobile-first, fully functional down to 375px viewports.
